🧠 Interview Insights
Why Kubernetes over Docker Compose? → Scaling, self‑healing, rolling updates.

How do you manage secrets? → Secret objects, not hardcoded env vars.

How do you expose services? → Service (ClusterIP, NodePort, LoadBalancer).

How do you monitor health? → Liveness/Readiness probes.
